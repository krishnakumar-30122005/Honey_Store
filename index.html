Honey And
Its Benefits

Honey is nature’s gift to mankind! A luscious liquid with a golden hue and bitter-sweet taste, it brings numerous health benefits in a small amount.

Honey has been praised in the Ayurveda scriptures, Ashtanga Hridayam and Charak Samhita, for its versatile medicinal properties and usage. Several shlokas explain the benefits of consuming honey, like lekhan (fat burner), shodhana (purification/detox), ropana (healing), laxative, yogavahi (drug carrier), and so on..

Today, we will closely look at – what are the benefits of eating honey regularly, its uses, and how to consume it in the right way.

Alright, let’s begin the journey of understanding honey and its benefits.
